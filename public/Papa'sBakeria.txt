# Papa’s Bakeria

Papa’s back with a brand new pie shop - Papa’s Bakeria! As your newly employed baker, your task is to bake various exquisite baked goods for customers. Choose a character and start! In Papa’s Bakeria, you can create your own custom character, too.

## How to Play Papa's Bakeria

### Fill, bake, and decorate

You wanted to land your dream job, but unfortunately, the shop closed, and you had to take a job at Papa Louie’s new Bakeria at Whiskfield Mall.

Roll up your sleeves and prepare for some accidental burns as you learn the basics of pie making by serving some customers under Papa’s supervision. After a while, you’ll be a Bakeria Boss, popping perfect pies out of the oven for your customers.

### Manage your time

As with all Papa’s games, time management is key. As you progress, there are more customers with more complex orders. You’ll have to balance these responsibilities well to keep your customers happy! Ensure you stay on top of the order station, build station, bake station, and top station for the best scores.

### Upgrade the Bakeria

Upgrades will also reduce the heat when the orders start flowing in. Use the money you’ve earned to buy upgrades to the cooking and building process. Various furnishings for the order station create an enjoyable waiting experience for your customers.

## Features

- Build a custom character in the character creator!
