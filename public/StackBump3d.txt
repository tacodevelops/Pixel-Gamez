# Stack Bump 3D

Stack Bump 3D is a quick, reflex-driven arcade game built around one simple idea: shoot down a stack of blocks before it shoots back at you. Tap or click to fire, clear the tower down to its last block, and move on to the next one — just don't hit black.

## How to Play

Each level presents you with a tower made up of colored blocks. Tapping or clicking fires at the tower, chipping away at it block by block until nothing's left, at which point you move on to a fresh tower and the process starts again.

The twist is the black blocks scattered throughout each tower. Hit one, and your run ends instantly, so the challenge isn't really about speed — it's about staying precise even as the pace picks up. Landing shots in a row builds a combo, and stacking enough hits together unlocks a short burst of unstoppable "fire mode," letting you tear through blocks without worrying about the usual restrictions for a few seconds.

Later towers add more black blocks and denser layouts, making it progressively harder to keep a clean streak going without breaking concentration.

## Controls

Tap or click to shoot. Holding down the input keeps you firing continuously.

## Features

- Simple one-input shooting mechanic
- A stack of levels with increasingly complex tower layouts
- Combo-based fire mode for temporary invincible shooting
- Fast, replayable arcade sessions

## Platform

Playable directly in the browser on both desktop and mobile.

## FAQ

**What happens if you hit a black block?**
Your run ends immediately, so avoiding them matters more than shooting quickly.

**How do you unlock fire mode?**
Landing a streak of consecutive hits builds a combo that triggers a short window of unrestricted shooting.
