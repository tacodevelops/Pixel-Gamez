# Snake.io

Snake.io drops you into a shared arena as a growing snake, racing other players to eat the most and survive the longest. Grow big enough and you stop being prey — instead, you become the thing everyone else on the map is trying to avoid.

## How to Play

The basics are simple: slither around the arena picking up scattered food to grow longer. The bigger you get, the more dangerous — and more of a target — you become.

**Speed and Risk**

Holding down the left mouse button lets your snake move faster, burning through some of the length you've built up in exchange for a quick burst of speed. That trade-off is central to the game: sprinting fast enough to cut off another player takes energy you can't get back instantly, so timing a burst of speed matters as much as having it available. Take down another snake and everything they were carrying gets scattered as food, giving you a big payout if you're quick enough to grab it.

**Skins**

A skins shop lets you customize your snake's appearance once you've unlocked enough of them. New skins come from hitting milestones like high scores or logging consecutive days played.

## Tips and Tricks

- Stay aggressive and take more risks early, while you're still small and have little to lose.
- Keep an eye out for the crown icon — it marks the largest snake currently on the map.
- Once you're big enough, try circling smaller snakes to box them in rather than chasing them directly.
- A large snake is intimidating on its own — sometimes the safest strategy is just to be too big to bother with.

## Controls

- **Mouse, WASD, or arrow keys** — Move
- **Hold left-click or spacebar** — Speed boost

## Features

- Real-time multiplayer snake battles
- Growth and speed mechanics tied to risk-reward tradeoffs
- Unlockable skins tied to in-game achievements
- Cross-platform play across browser, Android, and iOS

## Background

Snake.io was developed by Kooapps and originally released on iOS and Android in April 2016. A WebGL browser version followed later, launching in September 2022.

## Platforms

Playable in the browser, as well as on Android and iOS.
