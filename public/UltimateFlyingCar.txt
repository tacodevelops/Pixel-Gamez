# Ultimate Flying Car

Ultimate Flying Car is a simulation car game where you take fast rides and flights on the long streets of the city. Explore the sky with stylish flying cars, or unlock new cars by participating in races. You can play all of these game modes either 1-player or 2-player.

## Release Date

January 2023

## Developer

Ultimate Flying Car was developed by RHM Interactive.

## Platform

Web browser

## Last Updated

Nov 14, 2024

## Controls

**Player 1:**

- WASD = control car

- Shift = nitro

- T = look back

- R = fix car position

- Space = handbrake

**Player 2:**

- Arrow keys = control car

- M = nitro

- L = look back

- U = fix car position

- K = handbrake
