# House of Hazards

House of Hazards is a local multiplayer game where finishing a simple chore is anything but simple, because your housemates are actively trying to stop you. Make coffee, check the mail, water the plants — ordinary tasks turned chaotic by taps that misfire, lights that fall, and toast that flies through the air with genuine intent to harm.

## How to Play

The goal sounds mundane on paper: complete a short list of household tasks around the house. In practice, every task is a minefield, because the other players in the house are lurking nearby, ready to trigger a hazard the second you're in range. New players are better off starting in Time Trial mode first, just to get a feel for the controls and layout before other players get involved.

**Getting Things Done**

Progress comes from finishing tasks scattered around the house — small, everyday errands that would take seconds anywhere else. Here, every one of them is a chance for someone else to catch you off guard.

**Making Life Difficult for Everyone Else**

The flip side of the game is just as important. When you're not racing to finish your own tasks, you're free to sabotage everyone else's — firing off taps, flinging toast, triggering lasers, dropping lights on unsuspecting housemates, or even grabbing a fallen player and locking them away in a chest. Causing chaos is just as valid a strategy as staying focused.

**Players**

House of Hazards supports up to four players at once — two on keyboard, two on gamepad — or you can play solo against up to three AI-controlled opponents if you don't have a full house to fill out.

## Characters

The game starts you off with four standard characters — two male, two female:

- Dom
- Marissa
- Jerry
- Samantha

Additional characters unlock as you play and rack up wins:

- Robo Rob
- Buster
- Rocking Grandma
- Super Sam

## Controls

**Player 1**
- **A / D** — Move / cycle hazard selection
- **W** — Jump
- **S** — Crouch / grab a player / activate hazard

**Player 2**
- **J / L** — Move / cycle hazard selection
- **I** — Jump
- **K** — Crouch / grab a player / activate hazard

**Player 3–4 (Gamepad required)**
- **Left stick** — Move / cycle hazard selection
- **South button** — Jump
- **East button** — Crouch / grab a player / activate hazard

## Features

- Local multiplayer for up to 4 players (keyboard + gamepad support)
- Solo mode against AI opponents
- A mix of task-completion and active sabotage
- Eight playable characters, four of them unlockable

## Background

House of Hazards was released in September 2020 by New Eich Games, the studio also behind Rooftop Snipers and Getaway Shootout.

## Platform

Playable in the web browser.

## FAQ

**How do you unlock new characters in House of Hazards?**
New characters are unlocked by winning matches.

**Can you play House of Hazards online?**
No — it's a local multiplayer game, meant to be played with up to four people on the same device.
